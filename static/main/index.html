Hello!

 

This is EyeGee. It's called like that because it doing the same thing that iGoogle used to do so well and other personalized homepages around the internet don't - be simple and don't show ads.

 

Click the top-left button to add widgets. Drag them around to arrange your space - it's pretty straightforward. Check out the about page for more info.

 

I made EyeGee for myself. That's why it has only three widgets -
- the ones I need. But you can make your own because EyeGee is open source. Head over to the project page and see how. Install it on your server. Or just use this one.

 

If you decide to use this one, though, I can't guarantee backups and safety of your data. If something breaks, the worst thing that could happen is that you lose your notes and widget layout. This might be pretty bad, depending on what you decide to keep here, so, by all means, do not use it for important stuff ... yet!!!

 

Cheers.
